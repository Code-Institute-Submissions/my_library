Protagonist's Gender

A word of explanation about the Gender chart:

  • Male is a male protagonist and Female is a female main character, that much is obvious.
  • Multiple stands for multiple main characters of different genders. This applies when the story is told from multiple characters' point of view or the book is a collections of short stories.
  • None is a book without a protagonist, such as an encyclopedia, a historical account or other educational materials.
